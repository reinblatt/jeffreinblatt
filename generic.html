Cyber Security Community Resources

“The purpose of learning is growth, and our minds, unlike our bodies, can continue growing as we continue to live.” Mortimer Adler

The Cyber threat landscape is continually changing which makes continuous learning essential. Staying conscience of this and applying Cyber Security principles is essential in our personal and professional spheres. If we are not growing then we are on the path of regression. Let's stay vigilant and help those around us!

Resources

  • Security Weekly

    • Great for stay up to date on new security technology from folks who have been in the cyber security field for decades.

  • Building a Security Home Lab

    • Security Weekly folks provide a great resource for building a home lab. They cover Blue & Red Team & AppSec labs.

  • Black Hills Information Security

    • Fantastic resources from podcasts, training, job hunting, and keeping it real. Great for all levels. These people truly care about making cyber security fun.

  • How to build an Infosec Home Lab

    • Ralph May from Black Hills InfoSec provides a cool twist by covering hardware and software for homelabs.

  • SANS Internet Storm Center

    • Great daily podcast feed - short and to the point.

  • The Cyber Mentor

    • Focused on educating and helping people at all levels up their cyber security game.

  • Job Searching

    • For any of those who find themselves in the job hunting process. Check out Jason Blanchard out he provides some great help here in that space.
