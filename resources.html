Cyber Security Community Resources

“The purpose of learning is growth, and our minds, unlike our bodies, can continue growing as we continue to live.” Mortimer Adler

The Cyber threat landscape is continually changing which makes continuous learning essential. Staying conscience of this and applying Cyber Security principles is critical in our personal and professional lives. If we are not growing then we are on the path of regression. Let's stay vigilant and help those around us! Check out these resources...

Simply Cyber

Highly recommended for all security minded professionals. New and seasoned security professionals will find this to be a helpful resource.

  • Simply Cyber

Security Weekly

Great for staying up to date on new security technology from folks who have been in the cyber security field for decades.

  • Security Weekly

Black Hills Information Security

Fantastic resources from podcasts, training, job hunting, and keeping it real. Great for all levels. These people truly care about making cyber security fun.

  • Black Hills Information Security

SANS Internet Storm Center

Great Cyber Security daily podcast feed - short and to the point.

  • SANS Internet Storm Center

Builing Cyber Security Home Labs

Great resources for thinking through and building your own home security labs. Black Hills InfoSec & Security Weekly folks provide a great resource for building a home lab. They cover Blue & Red Team & AppSec labs.

  • Black Hills Home Labs
  • Security Weekly Home Labs

The Cyber Mentor

Heath focuses on educating and helping people at all levels up their cyber security game.

  • Cyber Mentor YouTube Channel

Job Hunting with Jason Blanchard from Black Hills InfoSec

For any of those who find themselves in the job hunting process. Check out Jason Blanchard out he provides some great help here in that space.

  • Hacking the Job Hunt Process

Red Canary - Learn Atomic Red Team

Red Canary is a great resource that is being innovative and providing the community with excellent open source content and training.
